About

Brydon Holsey

Graphic and web designer by day; Sailor, outdoor enthusiast, and mom by "night"

What makes a beautiful design is all relative to the viewer. A great designer can take the intangible and make it come to life.

I am inspired daily by the beautiful world and people we are surrounded by. I love using bright, vibrant colors and textures in illustrations, and strive to maintain a sleek, clean aesthetic while designing for the web. I enjoy working with people who see things differently than I do as it challenges me to think outside of the box and work with variables I may not have previously considered. Ideas constantly float around in my head about how to improve or fix a design, be it in my home, on my computer, or my daughters bright and terribly mismatched outfit.
